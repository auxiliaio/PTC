Who we are<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Promises Treatment Centre is a fully registered and Nacada accredited treatment and rehabilitation facility.<\/p>

It\u2019s a highly respected addiction recovery centre – renowned for breaking the cycle of addiction and restoring hope and health to individuals, families and communities. Our staff will put your culture, family and values at the center of your recovery.<\/p>

Ben Mumo, who is a trained and qualified addiction counselor, oversees all daily operations of PTC and ensures the quality of care and treatment provided is consistent and of the highest caliber. He draws on his personal experience with addiction and recovery to convey strength and hope to clients and their families.\u00a0<\/p>

Ben is committed to PTC\u2019s community of recovery and is a powerful advocate for the addict who still suffers. \u201cOur goal is for clients to leave PTC ready to live life feeling emotionally strong, personally accountable and excited about their ongoing recovery,\u201d he says. \u201cAt the core of our beliefs and values is our absolute conviction that all clients can succeed.\u201d<\/p>
